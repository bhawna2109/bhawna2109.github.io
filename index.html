My learnings from the process of building this website:

What started as a course project (Design of complex websites - Fall 2019) eventually evolved into this website! Looking back, the process of designing and building this website from scratch has been an extremely rewarding task for me. This project truly highlighted the iterative nature of design and underscored the importance of giving life to your designs early on. Being able to quickly bring what I had in my head on the screen helped me iterate faster. This experience has motivated me to take more advanced programming courses in Winter 2020, and make more interesting things!
