OVERVIEW

Project overview

Making workflows efficient is at the core of Postman's UX principles. Supporting tootips and autocomplete for variables is something that is present in all major development environments and it was long awaited in Postman.

MY ROLE

Responsibilities

As the only designer working on this feature, my responsibilities included defining the need, designing high-fidelity mocks, and writing documentation. Worked closely with engineers to ensure a high-quality implementation.
