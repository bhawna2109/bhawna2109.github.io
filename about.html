Hi there! I'm Bhawna (pronounces name bhaav-naa)

Nice to meet you! I am a user experience designer, passionate about crafting simple, intuitive, humane, engaging, and usable experiences that create a meaningful and a positive impact in the lives of people. I have been in the field of user experience design for more than 8 years now, but my appreciation and love for this field grows each day. It's exciting to see through experiences in day-to-day life wearing my 'oh, wait! there's a better way for this' lens!
