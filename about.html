Hi there! I'm Bhawna

Pronounces name bhaav-naa

Nice to meet you! I am a user experience designer, currently studying Human Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan. In the past, I have enjoyed crafting thoughtful experiences as a UX designer at Postman and Microsoft.

I am passionate about building things that create a meaningful and a positive impact in the lives of people. I have been in the field of user experience design for more than 8 years now, but my appreciation and love for this field grows each day. The most rewarding thing for me as a UX designer is being able to see through experiences in day-to-day life wearing my 'oh, wait! there's a better way for this' lens, and then collaborating with my friends to create simple, intuitive, humane, engaging, and usable experiences that could potentially change how millions - if not billions - of people get their job done. Always hungry to learn something new and augment my skills, I am a hard working individual who is naturally inquisitive and curious.

Currently thinking about:

  • How can we help people create and maintain their personal knowledge repository
  • Delving deep into the world of Artifical Intelligence and getting awed time and again
  • The future of interaction design

I'd love to hear from you!
