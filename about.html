Hi there! I'm Bhawna

Pronounces name bhaav-naa

Nice to meet you! I am a user experience designer, who thinks deeply, questions abundantly, designs thoughtfully, and likes to tell engaging stories. I am currently a Masters student studying Human Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan, where I am strengthening my design fundamentals, having fun with programming, and exploring new cultures. In the past, I have enjoyed crafting thoughtful experiences as a UX designer at Postman and Microsoft. As a UX designer in the industry, I enjoy the process of designing with constraints - both technological and business - in a dynamic and collaborative environment.

I love building things that create a meaningful and a positive impact in the lives of people. As an individual, I have always been fascinated by the evolution of technology and its impact on humans. My background in design and computer science - two incredible fields I am passionate about - helps me design experiences by understanding the user, and the technology. I believe that we need simpler, more intuitive, humane, engaging, and usable experiences for new innovations involving complex technology.
